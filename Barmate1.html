About Us

BarMate is an application that will use location based services to provide social opportunities for people who struggle to interact with others at a social venue.

Business to Consumer

BarMate is a platform with a broad collection of features for people and bar owners. Users are able to see menus, look at upcoming bar events, engage in a chat room with other people inside the bar, and more.

Business to Business

BarMate offers solutions for bar owners as well. A problem we’ve seen is that bar owners are not able to really obtain accurate data about things going on in their bar. BarMate’s solution to that is giving bar owners a way to see accurate data straight from BarMate that we call BarMate for Business.
